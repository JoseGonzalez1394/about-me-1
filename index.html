About Me

Angela DeLeon has been a creator since she was a kid. Whether it was through writing, construction or relationships she was always building something. As founder of Stagg Studio, she is now able to take those design, technical, and administration skills and develop software.

Angela studied at Portland Community College where she studied real estate, computer applications, and management. She graduated with a degree in general studies and seven program awards. She then attended Code Fellows and cemented her love of software development.

It is her goal to meet your goals.
